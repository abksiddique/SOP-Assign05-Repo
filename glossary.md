\# Glossary



\## Data Dictionary and Key Terms



\### Process Terms



\*\*Literature Review\*\*

Systematic process of identifying, evaluating, and synthesizing academic research relevant to a specific research question or topic area.



\*\*Search Query String\*\*

Combination of keywords and Boolean operators used to search academic databases for relevant publications.



\*\*Boolean Operators\*\*

Logical connectors (AND, OR, NOT) used to combine or exclude keywords in database searches to refine results.



\*\*Result Count Validation\*\*

Process of verifying that search results fall within acceptable range (100-5,000 papers) to ensure query is neither too narrow nor too broad.



\### Access \& Authentication Terms



\*\*Open Access\*\*

Research publications freely available online without subscription or payment barriers, indicated by green \[PDF] links without $ symbols.



\*\*Paywalled Content\*\*

Academic publications requiring subscription or payment to access, indicated by $ symbol or "Get access" text.



\*\*Institutional Login\*\*

Authentication system allowing university-affiliated researchers to access paywalled content through institutional subscriptions.



\*\*UC Credentials\*\*

University of Cincinnati login credentials formatted as 6-character username + 2-digit year (e.g., abcdef26).



\*\*VPN (Virtual Private Network)\*\*

Secure network connection allowing remote access to institutional resources as if on campus.



\### Technical Terms



\*\*PDF (Portable Document Format)\*\*

Standard file format for academic publications, ensuring consistent formatting across devices.



\*\*Citation\*\*

Bibliographic reference indicating a paper exists but may not be immediately accessible in full text.



\*\*ILL (Interlibrary Loan)\*\*

Service allowing researchers to request materials not available through their institution's subscriptions.



\### Workflow Terms



\*\*Exception Handling\*\*

Predefined procedures for addressing problems or failures in the standard process (e.g., login failures, VPN issues).



\*\*Escalation Path\*\*

Sequence of troubleshooting steps ending in advisor contact if technical issues cannot be self-resolved.



\*\*Naming Convention\*\*

Standardized format for saving files to ensure consistency and organization.



\### Research Context



\*\*Dissertation Research\*\*

Original scholarly investigation required for doctoral degree completion.



\*\*Anonymous Communication Networks\*\*

Network systems designed to protect user privacy and anonymity (context of the literature review SOP).



\*\*Google Scholar\*\*

Free academic search engine indexing scholarly literature across disciplines and formats.
